The New Space Economy Game
 
 

With companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, Planetary Resources and Deep Space Industries, and countless more taking to the skies, the future of humanity in space is brighter than ever! Players in Asteroid Ventures will be able to explore and colonize the solar system, or prepare ships to discover what lies beyond.

Work on Asteroid Ventures started as an open source simulation project for the NASA 2014 Space Apps Challenge. This project later recieved funding from SpaceGambit, to expand the capabilities of our project. Originally called Cosmosium, we have since switched from ThreeJS to Unreal Engine 4. This allows for faster iteration of development and more immersive graphics.

Players will colonize the Moon, setup large space stations, gain a foothold on Mars, capture Near-Earth Asteroids and create infrastrure in the Main Asteroid Belt. Some daring souls might even send derigibles to Venus and the gas giants!

With a focus on building a production chain in space or controlling trade through Orbit Cyclers, players must think tactically about technology, policy and prospecting. In addition, the interplay between private companies, national agencies, and new space-based sovereignties will play out by struggling to control trade-routes and territory by means of shareholder buyouts, embargoes, piracy and sabotage!
