The Cosmosium Engine
 
 

The Cosmosium Engine is an open source project started during the NASA 2014 Space Apps Challenge. This project later recieved funding from SpaceGambit, to expand the capabilities of our project.

The engine includes an interactive overview of the solar system with high-resolution planetary textures and level-of-detail optimizations. Thousands of minor planetary bodies generated from REAL ephemeris and asteroid shape data can be toggled. Spaceship models can be insterted into the scene and orbital transfers can be calculated through our API.

The Cosmosium Engine also features a GUI dashboard, tech tree library, and websockets implementation for real time server-client communication for communicating events.

A demonstration of the Cosmosium Engine in action is the Asteroid.Ventures web demo, wherein we push the limits of web app gaming to demonstrate parts of our grander game vision for Asteroid Ventures.
